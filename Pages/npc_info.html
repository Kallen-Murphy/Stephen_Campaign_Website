Crew

Chad Lackman

A former Member of the Saltmarsh Guard, Chad Lackman was the last surviving member of the force sent to assist the the party in the battle against the Monmurg Pirates. Folling the death of his brother-in-law during the aformentioned battle, Lackman joined the crew of the Sea Ghost in order to help rid the Sea of Swords of the Pirate Princes.

Drunky (Second Mate)

The town drunk of Saltmarsh, the man known to by the title "Second Mate" or by the nickname Drunky joined the crew on the offer of continuous access to the Sea Ghost's supply of alcohol in exchange for his labor.

Oceanus

Oceanus, a sea elf captured and enslaved by the Sea Prince Toli, was slated to be sold to the Sea Prince Hokar, captain of the casino ship and floating gladatorial arena known as The Luck of The Sea. He agreed to join the new Crew of the Sea Ghost followign Monmug's defeat in order to help defeat the Sea Prince Toli.

Sekar

The son of the Sea Prince Monmurg, Sekar came to hate his father folling the death of his mother at the pirate's hands. Sekar spent the majority of the time on the Sea Ghost under Monmurg as a prisoner of the Prince. Following the defeat of Monmurg by the party, Sekar gladly assisted the party, providing information on the Sea Princes and quickly joining the new crew of the Sea Ghost.

Tiny

Tiny, a man captured and enslaved by the Sea Prince Toli, was slated to be sold to the Sea Prince Hokar, captain of the casino ship and floating gladatorial arena known as The Luck of The Sea. He agreed to join the new Crew of the Sea Ghost followign Monmug's defeat in order to help defeat the Sea Prince Toli.

Allies

Anders Solmor

Anders Solmor is a young man who sits on Saltmarsh's Council. He has taken it upon himself to rid Saltmarsh of piracy and other crime. He has taken to sponsoring the actions of the party as they apear to have the greatest chance of freeing the village from the tyranny of the Sea Princes.

Weralt of Waterdeep

Weralt of Waterdeep is a bondsman hailing from the titular "City of Splendor" and a friend of Anders Solmor. He has been summoned to the city of Saltmarsh by Anders to train the City Guard following the disasterous battle against the crew of the Sea Prince Monmurg in the caves beneath the Ghost House.

Villians

Alfred Wavechaser

On the surface, Alfred Wavechaser is buttler and advisor of Anders Solmar; however, beneath the surface, he is working with the Sea Princes for some unknown reason,

Boe

Boe is a figure of unknown origins currently helping the Sea Prince Toli develop his new strange weapons technology.

Gailen Primewater

Gailen Primewater is a member of the Council of Saltmarsh and a member of the Scarlet Brotherhood. He has been working with the Sea Princes in the area presumably helping to provide them with some of the weapons produced by Wrenn Garric.

Hokar

Hokar is one of the four Sea Princes and the Captain of the Luck of The Sea, a casino ship and floading gladiatoral arena. He rutinely purchases slaves from the Sea Princes Toli and Monmurg.

Monmurg

Monmurg is the least significat of the four Sea Princes and the brother of Toli. He was the captain of the Sea Ghost before he was defeated by the party and imprisoned in Saltmarsh.

Toli

Toli is a member of the four Sea Princes with the two ships, The Persian and the Crimson Currier. The later is a smaller slave ship and the former is his primary vessel. He is currently in the pracess of developing a terifying new black powder capable of creating violent explosions when exposed to spark or fire along side his subordinate or ally Boe.

West Keep

West Keep is the greatest of the four Sea Princes. He has been working outside of the basic slave and drug trade of the other princes dealing in the trading of magical artifacts and the body parts of powerful monsters.
