What is Discord?

Discord is an online call and chat app which allows for the creation of dedicated servers for individual groups and comunities. Discord is available on all major platforms through dedicated apps in adition to a browser based web app. Discord offers a variety of comunication methods such as text, voice, and video chats in adition to other built in features such as screen sharing and the ability to create servers specific nicknames and roles for member. Furthur fucntionality can also be added through the use of outside bots added to the server such as music players.

How will Discord be used?

Discord will be the primary method of comunication between all members of the party including the Dungeon Master (DM) both in and outside of sessions. It will also serve to host a variety of content related to the game such as session notes and character and NPC art. If you are already a member of the server, you can access the server throught the link below or through the app. If you are not a member, you can view the server using the temporary invite link below under Check Out The Server.

How do I create an acount?

To create a Discord acount: go to the Discord website using google or the link below, click on the login button near the top right corner, and click on the register link placed directly below the login button.
