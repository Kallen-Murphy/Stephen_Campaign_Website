Major Locations

Saltmarsh

Saltmarsh is a small fishing vileage on a small island on the Sea of Swords, off the Sword Coast of Faerûn. The recent rise of the Sea Princes has placed Saltmarsh under a greater threat of piracy and other nefarious criminal activity.

Mount Hotenow

Mount Hotenow known to the ancient dwarves as Glaurimm, is the volcano prison of the Primordial Maegera which stands amid the Crags mountain range, situated to the northeast of the city of Neverwinter on the Sword Coast North, deep in Neverwinter Wood. It was home to a large population of fire elementals, warming the Neverwinter River to the point where it never froze over, even in the heart of winter. It is now host to the sulfur mines of the Sea Prince Toli.

Byat Byat

Byat Byat is the nere-surface lair of a group of the insaine god creating fish folk known as the Kua-toa which is being used by the Drow to sell enslaved peoples to the Sea Prince Toli and presumably other less savory individuals of the Sword Coast.
