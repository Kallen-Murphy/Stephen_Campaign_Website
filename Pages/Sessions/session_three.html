Session Three

Overview

The party defeated the Pirate Prince Monmurg taking him into custody along side the aprentice necromancer Punkatah. The party then preceded to prepair for the arival of Monmurg's brother, the Pirate Prince Toli, and his Crew where they learned of the potential betrail of Alfred Wavechaser. The party ended the session on the day of Toli's arival finishing there preperation.

Quests

Love letter

Deliver a letter from Anders Solmor to the daughter of the lord of Neverwinter Emeralda Neverember.

Major Events

The Defeat Of Monmurg

The battle against the monmurg pirates ended as a victory for the Party and the Saltmarsh guard but at a steep cost. with the party faced a great challenge in defeating the revealed to be werewolf captain Monmurg. Both the Pirate Prince Monmurg and the rogue aprentice Punkatah were captured along with the the Sea Ghost and the living members of its crew; however, the Saltmarsh guard took near complete casualties with only one member, Chad Lackman, surviving.

Toli's Arival

Two weeks following the Defeat of Monmurg and his crew, Toli arived one mile off of the coast of Saltmarsh in order to celebrate the birthday of his brother. The party finalized preparations for there attack on Toli and the Persian.

NPC Developments

The New Crew

Following the defeat of the Monmurg pirates, the party took the Sea Ghost as there own ship and recruited the prisoners on board the ship — Oceanus, Sekar, and Tiny — as members of crew in adition to the former guardsman Chad Lackman and the town Drunk, Drunky.

The Sea Princes

Upon the interigation of Monmurg, the party learned of the names of the four Sea Princes: Monmurg, Toli, Hokar, and West Keep. The great volcano of Mount Hotenow near the city of Neverwinter is currently serving as the main mining city of the magical sulfur known as "Devils Brimstone" that is being used to make the black powder.

Byat Byat, the lair of the the nere-surface lair of a group of the insaine god creating fish folk known as the Kua-toa is bing used by the Drow, specificaly the Drow Dimitri, to sell slaves to the Sea Princes.

The seemingly insignificant store Patricia's Wax Sticks serves as the main method of gaining access to the Luck of The Sea and the Sea Prince Hokar.
