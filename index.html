Setting

This campaign takes place in a modified version of the Forgoten Realms campaign setting in the area surounding Saltmarsh. A small fishing town on an island in the Sea of Swords near to the Sword Coast of the continent Faerûn. The campaign takes place during the mid 1490s DR, the primary time frame of Dungeons and Dragons Fifth Edition.
